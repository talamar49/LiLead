# LiLead User Guide

## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Register an Account**
   - Open the app
   - Tap "Register" on the login screen
   - Enter your name, email, and password
   - Tap "Register" to create your account

2. **Login**
   - Enter your email and password
   - Tap "Login"
   - You'll be taken to the Dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home screen, showing:
- **Statistics Cards**: Total leads, new leads, in process, and conversion rate
- **Leads by Status**: Pie chart showing distribution
- **Leads by Source**: Bar chart showing where leads come from

## Managing Leads

### Viewing Leads

### Adding a New Lead

1. Tap the **+** button on any lead list screen
2. Fill in the lead information:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Phone** (required)
   - **Email** (optional)
   - **Company** (optional)
   - **Value** (optional)
   - **Source** (select from dropdown)
3. Tap "Save" in the top right

### Lead Details

Tap any lead to view full details:

**Quick Actions:**
- **Call**: Opens phone dialer
- **WhatsApp**: Opens WhatsApp chat
- **Email**: Opens email client

**Lead Information:**
- Phone number
- Email address
- Company name
- Source
- Deal value

**Status Management:**
- Tap status chips to change lead status
- Available statuses: New, In Process, Closed, Not Relevant

**Notes:**
- View all notes with timestamps
- Add new notes using the text field at the bottom
- Tap send icon to save note

### Searching Leads

1. Navigate to any lead list screen
2. Use the search bar at the top
3. Type to filter leads by name, email, phone, or company
4. Results update in real-time

### Refreshing Data

- Pull down on any lead list to refresh
- Tap the refresh icon (üîÑ) in the app bar

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login
- Check your internet connection
- Verify email and password are correct
- Ensure backend server is running

### Leads Not Showing
- Pull down to refresh
- Check you're on the correct tab
- Verify backend connection

### WhatsApp/Call Not Working
- Ensure WhatsApp is installed for WhatsApp action
- Check phone permissions for calling
- Verify phone number format

### Search Not Working
- Clear search and try again
- Ensure you're searching in the correct field
- Pull to refresh data

### Theme Not Changing
- Restart the app after changing theme
- Check system theme if using "System" option

## Glossary

- **Lead**: A potential customer or business opportunity
- **Status**: Current stage of the lead (New, In Process, Closed, Not Relevant)
- **Source**: Where the lead came from (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of leads that become customers
- **RTL**: Right-to-Left (Hebrew text direction)
- **FAB**: Floating Action Button (the + button)
